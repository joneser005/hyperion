# hyperion
## Receive email notifications of movement detected by a webcam.

Requirements:
+ Python3 (3.6+)
+ motion

As configured, will attach 4 images, 1 old and 3 new, to an email shortly after motion is detected by the webcam.  Configure your email client to sound a special alert when you receive such emails.

Tailor the init bash shell script and your motion.conf file to suit your environment.
